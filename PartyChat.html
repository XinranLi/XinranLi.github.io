PartyChat!

Mobile UX/UI Design

 

PartyChat! An app that let you enjoy the best party with your best friends!

Clikable Mockup

Overview

PartyChat! is a social app designed for party boys and girls, and anyone who love parties!PartyChat! helps you to have fun with friends and organize party easily: all can be done via clicking the phone! What if you just want to create a private party that is not intended for everyone in your contact list? No problem! There is a grouping function that let you decide who can view this party and who can't. Once you have set up a party, a chatting room will be created automatically and other users will be notified. Everyone can discuss about the upcoming party and what they can do for help. You can also be invited or join parties and make new friends. After each party you can upload posts and photos and everyone who attend the party can view and make comments. Top posts that receive most likes and comments would be on the spotlight. PartyChat! is such an amazing app that can turn you into a social queen!

 

Problem Definition

Party is important in our social life and help people relax. But it is troublesome to organize a party. You need to invite everyone and make sure they have time to show up.

PEST Analysis

 
  • Politics To be decided
  • Economic Promote consumption when people get togather.
  • Social Connect people through party.
  • Technology Smartphone, Internet, Camera

Final Design

The main feature of our app is to remind people when they are supposed to get in touch with their family more frequently. We try to use an intuitive and interesting way to visualize how frequent people are contacting with their family.


Functions

  • Message chatting with friends and sharing videos and images
  • Party check out upcomming and past parties
